    • About Game

    Minecraft is an "open world" or sandbox construction video game originally created by Swedish Markus Persson (commonly known as "Notch"), and later developed by Mojang Studios (currently part of Microsoft). It was released on May 17. 2009, and after numerous changes, its first stable version "1.0" was released on November 18, 2011. The game focuses on the placement and destruction of blocks, being that this is made up of cubic three-dimensional objects, placed on a fixed grid pattern. These cubes or blocks mainly represent different elements of nature, such as earth, stone, minerals, trunks, among others.
