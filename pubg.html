    • About Game

    PUBG is an action video game in which up to 100 players fight in a Battle Royale, a type of deathmatch in which there are clashes to be the last one alive. Players can enter the game alone, or with a small team consisting of the player himself and one, two or three other people, respectively forming a duo, a squad of three or four players. The last person or team alive wins the game. Each game begins with players parachuting from a plane onto an island, to certain parts of their choosing. The game area is approximately 8 x 8 or 4 x 4 kilometers in size (this can vary depending on the size of the map); these start with no items other than the customizable clothing options they select before a match; all of the above from a plane that randomly flies over the map in a straight line. Once they land, players can search buildings and other locations or structures to find weapons, vehicles, armor, and other gear. These items are distributed throughout the map at the beginning of the game, with certain areas of high risk, but with better equipment. These become hidden to avoid being killed or hunting other players while they continue to search for more equipment. Killed players can be looted to acquire their equipment.
