    • About Game

    Marvel Future Revolution begins when numerous Earths converge in the multiverse to form an entirely new "Prime Earth" filled with unique zones and quests to explore, such as the high-tech city New Stark City, the dangerous Hydra Empire, the wild and rugged Sakaar, and many more. As agents of the newly formed "Omega Flight" superhero team, players will work together to fight off an onslaught of super-villains and defend "Prime Earth" from an endless array of threats. is an MMORPG game that combines traditional elements of both action games and open world games. The result is a title full of action, in which the player can move freely through huge scenarios in the company of other players. It can be played both with friends and with other random players. The control system is very reminiscent of action-focused games, especially most MOBAs. On the left side of the screen, we will have the virtual movement "stick", with which we can move our superhero; and on the right side, we will have all the action buttons, with which we can attack and carry out our special abilities. Each of the characters in the game has completely different abilities than the others. Captain America, for example, allows you to play in a totally different way than Star-Lord or Spider-Man. Learning to master the best skills is essential to emerge victorious in the most difficult confrontations. Marvel Future Revolution has a story mode that has narrative scenes in which you can discover what is happening throughout the Marvel Universe. Actions and victories are what move the story forward. Also the more you advance, the more levels you will need to have. Featuring eight playable superheroes, including Black Widow, Captain America, Captain Marvel, Spider-Man, Iron Man, Doctor Strange, Star-Lord and Storm, they are outfitted in an extensive collection of iconic costumes with over 400 million combinations.
