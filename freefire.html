    • About Game

    Garena Free Fire is a battle royale action-adventure game played in the third person. The game consists of up to 50 players parachuting onto an island in search of weapons and equipment to kill the other players. Players are free to choose their starting position, pick up weapons and supplies to extend their life in battle. When players join a match, they enter a plane that flies over the island. As the plane flies over the island, players can jump wherever they want, allowing them to choose a strategic spot to land away from enemies. After landing, players must go in search of weapons and useful items. Medical equipment, medium and large weapons, grenades, and other featured items can be found all over the island. The ultimate goal of the players is to survive on the island with a maximum of 50-51 players online; For this, it is necessary to eliminate all the adversaries that the players find along the way and make sure that they are the only survivor left. The safe zone available on the game map decreases in size over time, driving surviving players into tighter zones to force encounters. The last player or team standing wins the round. The game had received a major update on Monday, December 7, 2020,11 which brought changes to the training ground as it introduced some updated weapons. The game has received the next major patch update on April 14, 2021 which introduced the new Bermuda Remastered map permanently and a new Kord in-game weapon in this update.
