    • About Game

    Asphalt Nitro 2 is basically Asphalt but for lower end devices. The developers have stated that the game features top-notch graphics, 20 licensed supercars (including brands like Bugatti, Ferrari, and Lamborghini), four arcade game modes, and 230 races. The tracks are set in wonderful places in Japan and New Zealand. Gameloft Business Solutions is pleased to present its new mobile game: Asphalt Nitro 2. Since the release of its predecessor in 2015, there has never been a more intense racing game for mobile devices that offers so much excitement in such a compact package.
