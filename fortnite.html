    • About Game

    Fortnite is a world of multiple experiences. Drop onto the island and compete until you are the last players (or teams) standing. Create a custom island with your own rules. Hang out with your friends on an island created by one of your friends. Or eliminate hordes of monsters with other players to save the world. In Fortnite Battle Royale, get off the Battle Bus, drop onto the island and fight until no one else is left standing. You can also team up with your friends or other players to outlast your opponents. Use wood, brick and metal as materials to build structures to help you survive. There is always something to do in Fortnite Battle Royale: new items, weapons, vehicles, modes, special events and seasons. Each new season brings a distinctive theme to the island, such as the domination of secret agents or a massive flood. Currently, you can download Fortnite Battle Royale, Zero Build, and Creative on PlayStation, Xbox, Nintendo Switch, PC, and the Epic Games app for Android via Fortnite.com/Android or the Galaxy Store on Fortnite. If you want to play the new Fortnite updates, you can download the game on any of the other platforms listed above.
